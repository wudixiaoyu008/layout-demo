Healthy Chocolate Mocha Smoothie

8 min Serves 2 Gluten-free

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened, vanilla almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 8 unsweetened, vanilla almond milk ice cubes (just freeze the milk in an ice cube tray the night before - any milk works)
  • ¼ large avocado
  • 5-6 pitted dates
  • 2 tablespoons cacao powder or unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons cold brewed coffee concentrate
  • 2 tablespoons hemp hearts
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds or flaxseed
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla bean powder or 1 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in blender and puree until smooth and creamy ensuring the dates have been fully incorporated and no chunks remain.
  2. Pour into glasses and enjoy!
